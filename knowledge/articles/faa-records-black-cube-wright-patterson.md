## Overview

Newly released FAA documents obtained under FOIA case 2025-04622 add significant new information to the story about unauthorized drone incursions at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base in December 2024.

## Key Details

- **Drone Swarm:** Up to 17 drones buzzed sensitive areas of the base
- **Lockdown:** Base went on lockdown during the incidents
- **Black Cube Sighting:** Commercial flight at 16,000 ft reported a "black cube" object whizzing 500 ft beneath them
- **Confirmation:** FAA radar, pilots, and base security all confirmed the drone activity
- **Omission:** The Air Force left out the black cube detail in their earlier reports
