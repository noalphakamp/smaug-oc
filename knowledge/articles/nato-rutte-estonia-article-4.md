# NATO chief clashes with Estonia over Article 4 response to Russia

**Source:** Fox News  
**URL:** https://www.foxnews.com/politics/nato-allies-clash-after-russian-jets-breach-airspace-testing-alliance-resolve.amp  
**Date:** October 3, 2025  
**Author:** Fox News Politics

## Summary

Secretary General Mark Rutte clashed with Estonian Prime Minister Kristen Michal over NATO's Article 4 invocations following Russian airspace incursions, according to sources.

## Key Points

- Rutte argued that if Article 4 were invoked every time Russia violated sovereignty, it would quickly lose impact
- Estonia doesn't have an air force to respond to Russian MiG-31s
- NATO internal tensions exposed over response to Russian aggression
