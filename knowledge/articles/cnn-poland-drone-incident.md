## Overview

CNN reporting on Poland scrambling jets after reports of Russian drones entering Polish airspace near the Ukrainian border. The incident represents a significant escalation in the Russia-Ukraine conflict spilling over into NATO territory.

## Key Details

- **Location:** Eastern Poland, near Rzeszów and Zamość, close to Ukrainian border
- **Threat:** At least three "Shahed" attack drones reportedly downed
- **Response:** NATO Quick Reaction Alert (QRA) aircraft engaged
- **Affected Regions:** Podlaskie, Mazowieckie, and Lubelskie voivodeships
- **Military Action:** Polish Armed Forces deployed weaponry and conducted neutralization operations

## Significance

This incident marks a serious escalation with potential NATO Article 5 implications. Russian drones violating Polish airspace could be:
- Deliberate tests of NATO response capabilities
- Navigational errors from Russia's aging drone fleet
- Intentional probing of allied defenses

## Polish Response

The Operational Command of the Polish Armed Forces confirmed the airspace violations and stated that military operations remain ongoing. Residents in affected areas were advised to stay at home.
