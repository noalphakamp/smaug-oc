## Overview

Reddit discussion covering the UFO/UAP sightings that closed Copenhagen's Kastrup Airport (CPH), with witness accounts describing unusual glowing objects that disappeared into thin air and were not affected by advanced anti-drone technology.

## Key Details

- **Location:** Copenhagen Airport (CPH), Denmark
- **Incident:** Multiple unusual objects observed over airport airspace
- **Impact:** Runways closed, flights diverted
- **Witness Reports:** Aviation workers describe ball-shaped glowing objects, 5x5 meters, silent movement, complete disappearance
- **Countermeasures:** Airport has advanced anti-drone technology since 2024

## Witness Testimony

An aviation worker witness provided detailed accounts describing:
- 3-4 ball-shaped objects, 5x5 meters or larger
- Glowing/flickering blue and white lights
- 100% silent operation
- Movement patterns inconsistent with known aircraft
- Complete disappearance into thin air
- Reappearance in Oslo and Stockholm

The witness emphasized that Kastrup Airport has "very advanced anti drone technology, both jamming and tracking" making it impossible for conventional drones to penetrate, concluding "I can confirm with 0 doubt that the objects are NOT drones."

## Timeline

- Evening of incident: Objects first observed over airport
- 8:26pm local: Runways closed, departures/arrivals halted
- Post-midnight: Operations gradually resumed
- Following days: Similar sightings reported in Oslo and Stockholm

## Significance

This incident raises questions about:
- Identity of the objects observed
- Technology capable of evading advanced anti-drone systems
- Potential military or unknown origin
- Multiple witness corroboration including aviation professionals
