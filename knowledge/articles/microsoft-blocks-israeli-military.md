Microsoft has blocked the Israeli military from accessing certain cloud and AI services, according to reports. The decision follows revelations in The Guardian last month that Microsoft's services were being used for mass surveillance of Palestinians.

## Key Takeaways

- Microsoft restricts Israeli military access to cloud infrastructure and AI tools
- Action follows Guardian investigation into surveillance operations
- Part of broader scrutiny on tech companies' involvement in military and intelligence activities
