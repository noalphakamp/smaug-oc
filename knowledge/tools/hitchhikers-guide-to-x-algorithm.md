A comprehensive analysis of the recently open-sourced X algorithm. Key insights include: the Grok transformer encodes your last 128 engagement actions to predict future behavior; likes are the primary ranking signal; negative feedback (blocks, mutes, reports, "not interested") actively suppresses content; posting multiple times per day results in exponentially decaying score multipliers. Repository includes actionable insights, signal weights, scoring formulas, and diversity breakdowns.
